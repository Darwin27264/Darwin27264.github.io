TranslatoX for Google Slides™

Free Translation for Your Presentation Needs.

translatoX for Google Slides™ enables you to connect with a global audience by effortlessly translating your presentations. Break language barriers and reach more people with the click of a button, ensuring your message resonates with diverse viewers worldwide.

 

 

Enjoy the Benefits

  • Instant translation of slide content
  • Support for multiple languages
  • Easy-to-use interface within Google Slides™
  • Preserves slide formatting and layout
  • Powered by the reliable Google Translate™ API

Get Started

  1. Install the translatoX for Google Slides™ plugin from the Google Workspace Marketplace™.
  2. Open your Google Slides™ presentation.
  3. Select the slides you want to translate.
  4. Choose the desired target language.
  5. Click the "Translate" button, and let translatoX take care of the rest.
