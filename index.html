Background:

Hi! I'm currently a 2nd year student at Queen's University studying Computer Science and Economics. I was born in Shanghai, China, and lived there for 10 years before moving to Fredericton, Canada. I currently live in Kingston, Ontario, where I am attending Queen's University. I am passionate about programming and loves to invest time into personal projects and readings that may help me build my skills.

Self-motivated and excited to learn about the endless possibilities of technologies, I have been, and still am amazed at what computers are able to achieve ever since I wrote my first Hello World program. Fueled by curiosity and the goal of providing solutions and aid to others, I wish to self-improve ever since to make it what I will do in my professional life. Some "Non CS" related hobbies of mine includes music production, aerial photography with my drone, video editing, playing instruments, design/art.

Currently:

Currently, I am working in the role of a web manager at Pathfinders Christian Fellowships, an organization that aims to help make readings of different religions more accessible to people in a concise and well-rounded package, hosted in a series of websites in the form of basic websites to PWAs.

Aside from participating in Game Jams and Hackathons, I am also a member of the biggest student ran AI club in Canada, QMIND, where I have worked on various AI related tasks.
